<p><p>The intelligent sorting of express parcels involves various technologies to enhance efficiency in logistics. Cross belt sorters utilize a series of wide belts to move items quickly and accurately to designated locations. Shoe sorters use angled shoes to push parcels off the track, suitable for smaller items. Tilt tray sorters employ trays that tilt to the side, allowing for precise item placement. Additionally, other methods may include robotic systems and automated guided vehicles, optimizing sorting processes in diverse operations.</p></p>
