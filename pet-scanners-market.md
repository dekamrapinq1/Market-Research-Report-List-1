<p><strong>The PET Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PET Scanners (Only Including PET)</li><li>PET-CT Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET scanners market can be categorized into two main types: standalone PET scanners and PET-CT scanners. Standalone PET scanners focus solely on detecting metabolic processes using positron emission tomography, providing detailed images of organ function. In contrast, PET-CT scanners combine PET imaging with computed tomography to offer enhanced anatomical localization alongside metabolic information. This integration improves diagnostic accuracy, making PET-CT particularly valuable in oncology, neurology, and cardiology, where precise imaging is crucial for effective treatment planning.</p></p>
