<p><strong>The Convertible Bond Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vanilla Convertible Bond</li><li>Mandatory Convertible Bond</li><li>Reversible Convertible Bond</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The convertible bond market consists of various types, each with distinct features. Vanilla convertible bonds allow holders to convert bonds into a predetermined number of shares at a fixed price. Mandatory convertible bonds automatically convert into equity after a set term, providing less flexibility to investors but typically higher yields. Reversible convertible bonds, on the other hand, start as debt instruments but allow issuers to convert them back to bonds after a specified period, offering strategic investment options for both parties.</p></p>
