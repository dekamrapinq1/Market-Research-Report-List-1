<p><p>The Video on Demand (VOD) software market comprises various types tailored to user preferences. Transactional Video on Demand (TVOD) allows viewers to pay for individual titles, offering flexibility. Subscription Video on Demand (SVOD) provides access to a vast library for a monthly fee. Near-Video on Demand (NVOD) schedules content for specific times, similar to traditional television but with options for viewers to catch specific shows. Other models include ad-supported platforms and free video services, catering to diverse audience needs.</p></p>
