<p><ul><li>Vitamin D-Related Rickets</li><li>Hypocalcemia-Related Rickets</li><li>Hypophosphatemia-Related Rickets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rickets market is categorized into three main types based on underlying deficiencies. Vitamin D-related rickets occurs due to inadequate vitamin D, leading to poor calcium absorption. Hypocalcemia-related rickets results from low calcium levels, which can also weaken bones. Hypophosphatemia-related rickets is caused by insufficient phosphate, crucial for bone mineralization. Each type has specific treatment options, impacting market dynamics, including pharmaceuticals, dietary supplements, and public health initiatives aimed at prevention and management of these conditions.</p></p>
