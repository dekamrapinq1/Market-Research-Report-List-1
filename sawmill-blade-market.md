<p><strong>The Sawmill Blade Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circular Saw Blade</li><li>Band Saw Blade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sawmill blade market is primarily divided into two types: circular saw blades and band saw blades. Circular saw blades are designed for precise, straight cuts, making them ideal for processing large logs into lumber. They feature a rotating disc shape, allowing for high-speed cutting. In contrast, band saw blades consist of a long, continuous strip of metal with teeth along one edge, suitable for making intricate cuts and reducing waste while handling thicker materials. Both blade types play crucial roles in enhancing efficiency and accuracy in sawmill operations.</p></p>
