<p><p>Bromide ion selective electrodes (ISEs) are essential tools for measuring bromide ion concentrations in various applications. The market for these electrodes can be categorized into three types: crystal membrane, liquid membrane, and PVC membrane. Crystal membrane ISEs utilize solid ionic crystals, offering high stability and selectivity. Liquid membrane electrodes contain a liquid phase that selectively allows bromide ions to pass through, while PVC membrane electrodes utilize a polymer matrix that provides an alternative selective medium, balancing performance, ease of use, and cost.</p></p>
