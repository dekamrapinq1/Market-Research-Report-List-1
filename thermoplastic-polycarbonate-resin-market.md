<p><p>Thermoplastic polycarbonate resin is primarily categorized into two market types: extrusion grade and molding grade. Extrusion grade is designed for continuous processes, allowing the material to be formed into sheets, films, and profiles, offering transparency and impact resistance. Molding grade, on the other hand, is optimized for injection and blow molding applications, ideal for producing complex shapes and durable components. Both grades serve diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and consumer goods, due to their versatility and performance characteristics.</p></p>
