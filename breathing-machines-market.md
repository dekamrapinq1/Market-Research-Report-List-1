<p><p>The breathing machines market encompasses various devices catering to respiratory health. Asthma nebulizers turn liquid medication into mist for easier inhalation, helping asthma patients manage symptoms. PAP (Positive Airway Pressure) machines assist individuals with sleep apnea by maintaining airflow during sleep. Oxygen concentrators filter ambient air to deliver concentrated oxygen for patients with chronic respiratory issues. Ventilators are critical in hospitals, providing mechanical support for patients unable to breathe adequately on their own, ensuring effective oxygenation and ventilation.</p></p>
