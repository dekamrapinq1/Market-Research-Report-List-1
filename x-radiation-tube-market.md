<p><strong>The X-Radiation Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode</li><li>Rotating Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Radiation Tube market is primarily categorized into two types: stationary anode and rotating anode tubes. Stationary anode tubes utilize a fixed anode target that can limit heat dissipation, making them suitable for low-power applications. In contrast, rotating anode tubes feature a rotating target, allowing for better heat management and enabling higher radiation output. This makes them ideal for high-performance imaging and diagnostic applications, providing enhanced efficiency and image quality across various medical and industrial uses.</p></p>
