<p><p>The UV Offset Inks market comprises two primary types: sheet-fed offset and web-fed offset. Sheet-fed offset involves printing on individual sheets of paper, allowing for high-quality finishes and versatility in various print applications. In contrast, web-fed offset uses continuous rolls of paper, making it more suitable for high-volume printing projects like magazines and newspapers. Both types leverage UV curing technology to enhance drying speeds and print durability, catering to diverse industries and printing needs.</p></p>
