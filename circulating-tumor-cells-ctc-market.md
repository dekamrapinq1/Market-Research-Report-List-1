<p><p>Circulating Tumor Cells (CTCs) market encompasses various technologies for cancer diagnostics. Ex-Vivo Positive Selection focuses on isolating CTCs from the bloodstream after sample collection, enhancing detection through magnetic or affinity-based methods. In-Vivo Positive Selection captures CTCs directly within the body, often using specialized devices. Negative Selection enriches CTCs by removing non-target cells, improving purity. Microchips and Single Spiral Micro Channels are advanced platforms for efficient CTC capture and analysis, leveraging microfluidics to enhance sensitivity and specificity in detection processes.</p></p>
