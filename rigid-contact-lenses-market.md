<p><p>The rigid contact lenses market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on Rigid Gas Permeable (RGP) lenses and Orthokeratology (Ortho-K) lenses. RGP lenses are durable, offering excellent vision correction and oxygen permeability, making them suitable for those with astigmatism or keratoconus. Ortho-K lenses are specialized rigid lenses designed to temporarily reshape the cornea overnight, providing clear vision during the day without the need for glasses or contact lenses. Both types cater to specific vision correction needs and preferences among consumers.</p></p>
