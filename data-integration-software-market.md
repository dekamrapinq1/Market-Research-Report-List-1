<p><p>Data integration software is categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and hosted solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing complete control over data management and security but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, hosted solutions are cloud-based, accessible via the internet, and often offer easier scalability and reduced infrastructure costs. They allow organizations to leverage the provider's resources and expertise, making them appealing for businesses seeking flexibility and lower maintenance burdens.</p></p>
