Water fire aircraft are designed to scoop water from bodies of water and drop it on fires, ideal for combating wildfires and large-scale blazes. In contrast, land firefighting aircraft are typically equipped for aerial support in more localized settings, delivering fire retardants or extinguishing agents directly to ground operations. Both types play crucial roles in modern firefighting strategies.</p></p>
