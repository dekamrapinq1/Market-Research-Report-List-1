<p><p>The ABS resin processing market is primarily divided into two main types: injection molding grade and extrusion grade. Injection molding grade ABS is designed for producing intricate parts with tight tolerances, offering excellent surface finish and mechanical properties, making it suitable for applications in automotive, consumer goods, and electronics. On the other hand, extrusion grade ABS is utilized for continuous processes to create sheets, films, and pipes, valued for its impact resistance and ease of processing, catering to packaging and construction industries.</p></p>
