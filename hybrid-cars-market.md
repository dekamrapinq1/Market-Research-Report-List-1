<p><strong>The Hybrid Cars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Hybrid</li><li>Parallel Hybrid</li><li>Plug-in Hybrid</li><li>Series-Parallel Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid cars can be classified into four main market types based on their design and functionality. </p><p>**Series hybrids** use a gasoline engine to generate electricity for an electric motor, with the wheels powered solely by the motor. </p><p>**Parallel hybrids** use both an internal combustion engine and an electric motor to drive the wheels, allowing for flexible power sources. </p><p>**Plug-in hybrids** feature larger batteries and can be charged via external sources, enabling longer electric-only driving ranges.</p><p>**Series-parallel hybrids** combine both configurations, allowing for optimized efficiency and performance.</p></p>
