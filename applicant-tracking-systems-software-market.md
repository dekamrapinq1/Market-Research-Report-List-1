<p><p>Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) software is categorized into two primary market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise ATS is installed locally on a company’s servers, offering control over data and customization, but requiring significant IT resources and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based ATS is hosted online, allowing for easy access from anywhere, scalability, and lower upfront costs. It typically includes regular updates and support, making it more flexible for evolving business needs, especially for companies with remote or diverse workforces.</p></p>
