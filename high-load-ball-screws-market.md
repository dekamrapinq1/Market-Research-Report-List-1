<p><p>The high-load ball screws market primarily comprises two types: rolled and ground ball screws. Rolled ball screws are manufactured through a rolling process, offering cost-effectiveness and suitable for applications requiring moderate precision and load capabilities. In contrast, ground ball screws are produced through precision grinding, resulting in superior accuracy and efficiency, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, such as machine tools, robotics, and aerospace, where reliability and high load-carrying capacity are essential.</p></p>
