<p><ul><li>PTC Heater</li><li>Heat Pump Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Heating System market primarily includes two types: PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) heaters and heat pump heaters. PTC heaters utilize electrical resistance to generate heat quickly, providing rapid cabin warming, while being compact and efficient. On the other hand, heat pump heaters operate by transferring heat from the outside air to the vehicle's interior, offering higher efficiency in moderate temperatures and reducing energy consumption. Each type caters to different climate conditions and consumer preferences, enhancing overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
