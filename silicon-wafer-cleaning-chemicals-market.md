<p><strong>The Silicon Wafer Cleaning Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Cleaning</li><li>Hydrofluoric Acid Dip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon wafer cleaning chemicals market encompasses two primary types: solvent cleaning and hydrofluoric acid dip. Solvent cleaning involves using organic solvents to remove contaminants such as oils and residues from wafers, ensuring a clean surface for further processing. In contrast, hydrofluoric acid dip is employed to eliminate native oxides from silicon wafers, providing a pristine surface essential for facilitating improved adhesion during subsequent fabrication steps. Both methods are critical in maintaining the quality and performance of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
