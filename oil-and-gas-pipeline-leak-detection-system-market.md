<p><strong>The Oil and Gas Pipeline Leak Detection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acoustic/Ultrasonic</li><li>E-RTTM</li><li>Fiber-Optic</li><li>Vapor Sensing</li><li>Mass/Volume Balance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil and gas pipeline leak detection system market comprises several technologies. Acoustic/ultrasonic systems detect leaks through sound wave anomalies. E-RTTM (Real-Time Thermal Monitoring) utilizes temperature changes to identify leaks. Fiber-optic sensors measure changes in light transmission along a pipeline. Vapor sensing systems detect gas emissions resulting from leaks, while mass/volume balance techniques compare the input and output of fluid to identify discrepancies indicative of leaks. Each technology offers unique advantages for pipeline monitoring and safety.</p></p>
