<p><p>The packaging plastic tube market comprises several key materials, each with distinct properties. Polypropylene (PP) offers high clarity and chemical resistance, suitable for food and personal care products. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) is versatile and can be rigid or flexible, often used in pharmaceuticals. Polyester (PET) is known for its strength and recyclability, commonly used in beverage packaging. Other materials include unconventional plastics and bioplastics.</p></p>
