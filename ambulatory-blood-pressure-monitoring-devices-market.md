<p><p>Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitoring (ABPM) devices are categorized into two main types: Ordinary ABPM and Mobile-based ABPM. Ordinary ABPM typically involves traditional devices that measure blood pressure at regular intervals over 24 hours, providing comprehensive data on a patient's blood pressure patterns. In contrast, Mobile-based ABPM utilizes advanced technology, integrating smartphone applications for real-time monitoring and data analysis. This type offers convenience and accessibility, allowing patients to track their blood pressure on-the-go while encouraging adherence to monitoring protocols.</p></p>
