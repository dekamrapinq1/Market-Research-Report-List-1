<p><p>The hydrogen-powered tractor market features two main types: hydrogen power and dual power. Hydrogen-powered tractors operate entirely on hydrogen fuel cells, offering zero emissions and sustainable operation. In contrast, dual power tractors utilize both hydrogen and diesel, providing flexibility and extended range while reducing overall emissions. This dual approach allows farmers to gradually transition to greener technologies without sacrificing power or efficiency, making it an appealing option for those hesitant to fully commit to hydrogen alone.</p></p>
