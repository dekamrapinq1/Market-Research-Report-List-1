<p><p>The industrial pneumatic caster market comprises two primary types: rigid casters and swivel casters. Rigid casters have fixed wheel orientations, providing stability and load-bearing capacity for straight-line movement, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, swivel casters feature a rotating wheel assembly, allowing for greater maneuverability and control in tight spaces. Both types utilize pneumatic wheels, which enhance shock absorption and traction, making them suitable for various industrial environments, including warehouses and manufacturing facilities.</p></p>
