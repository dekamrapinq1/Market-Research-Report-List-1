<p><ul><li>Gridded Ion Engine (GIE)</li><li>Hall Effect Thruster (HET)</li><li>High Efficiency Multistage Plasma Thruster (HEMPT)</li><li>Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric propulsion satellite market encompasses various technologies, including Gridded Ion Engines (GIE), which use electric fields to accelerate ions for thrust; Hall Effect Thrusters (HET), which rely on magnetic fields to ionize and accelerate propellant; High Efficiency Multistage Plasma Thrusters (HEMPT), which provide high thrust and efficiency through multiple stages; and Pulsed Plasma Thrusters (PPT), known for their simplicity and lightweight design, utilizing electrical pulses to expel plasma for propulsion. Each type offers unique benefits for satellite operations.</p></p>
