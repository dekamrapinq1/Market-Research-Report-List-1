<p><ul><li>Cold-Pressed Walnut Oil</li><li>Expeller-Pressed Walnut Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The walnut oil market primarily consists of two types: cold-pressed and expeller-pressed walnut oil. Cold-pressed walnut oil is extracted through a low-temperature process, preserving its flavor, nutrients, and delicate aroma, making it ideal for culinary uses and gourmet applications. In contrast, expeller-pressed walnut oil is obtained using a mechanical pressing method at higher temperatures, which can enhance extraction efficiency but may compromise some flavor and nutritional quality. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and applications in cooking and cosmetics.</p></p>
