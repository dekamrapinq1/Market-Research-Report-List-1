<p><p>The ultra-capacitor market for electric buses features three main types: electrochemical double-layer capacitors (EDLCs), hybrid capacitors, and pseudo capacitors. EDLCs store energy through electrostatic charge separation, offering high power density and rapid charge/discharge cycles. Hybrid capacitors combine features of EDLCs and batteries, providing a balance of energy and power density. Pseudo capacitors utilize fast redox reactions to enhance energy storage, benefiting applications requiring higher energy density. Together, these technologies enhance performance and efficiency in electric bus systems.</p></p>
