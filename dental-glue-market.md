<p><p>The dental glue market comprises two main types: temporary dental glue and permanent dental glue. Temporary dental glue is designed for short-term use, often employed in emergency dental procedures or to secure temporary restorations until a permanent solution is available. In contrast, permanent dental glue provides a long-lasting bond for dental restorations, implants, and prosthetics, ensuring durability and stability. Both types play essential roles in dental practices, catering to different patient needs and treatment timelines.</p></p>
