<p><p>The Silicon Carbide Fine Powder market is primarily categorized into two types: α-SiC and β-SiC. α-SiC, known for its hexagonal crystal structure, exhibits superior hardness and thermal stability, making it suitable for applications in abrasive materials and cutting tools. In contrast, β-SiC features a cubic crystal structure, providing excellent electronic and optical properties, ideal for semiconductor devices and high-temperature applications. Both types fulfill distinct industrial needs, driving innovation and growth in sectors such as electronics, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
