<p><p>The semi-metallic industrial gaskets market includes various types tailored for specific applications. Soft gaskets are compressible and ideal for irregular surfaces. Spiral wound gaskets combine soft and metal materials for enhanced sealing. Ring joint gaskets provide high-pressure sealing in flanged joints. Kammprofile gaskets offer flexibility and strength with a metal core and soft sealing surfaces. Jacketed gaskets feature an outer metal jacket for durability, while corrugated gaskets provide improved compression and adaptability. Other types serve niche applications across industries.</p></p>
