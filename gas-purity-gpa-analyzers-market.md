<p><p>Gas purity analyzers (GPA) are essential instruments used for measuring the purity of gases in various applications. The market is divided into two primary types: benchtop and floor-standing analyzers. Benchtop GPAs are compact, portable units suited for laboratory environments, offering flexibility in testing. In contrast, floor-standing analyzers are larger, often integrated into industrial settings, designed for continuous monitoring and enhanced accuracy in high-volume processes. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring the quality and safety of industrial gases.</p></p>
