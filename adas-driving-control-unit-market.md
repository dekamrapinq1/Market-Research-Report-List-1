<p><p>The ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) Driving Control Unit market is segmented into three key types: </p><p>1. **Perception Control Unit**: This unit processes sensor data to interpret the vehicle's surroundings, identifying obstacles, lane markers, and road signs.</p><p>2. **Decision Control Unit**: This component uses information from the perception unit to make driving decisions, such as speed adjustments or lane changes.</p><p>3. **Actuator Control Unit**: This unit executes the decisions made by the decision control unit by controlling the vehicle's steering, acceleration, and braking systems, ensuring a safer driving experience.</p></p>
