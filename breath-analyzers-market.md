<p><strong>The Breath Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fuel Cell</li><li>Semiconductor</li><li>Infrared</li><li>Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Breath analyzers are categorized into several market types based on their sensing technologies. Fuel cell analyzers utilize electrochemical reactions to detect alcohol levels, providing high accuracy. Semiconductor analyzers use metal oxide sensors that change resistance with gas exposure, offering cost-effective solutions. Infrared analyzers employ spectroscopic techniques to measure gas concentrations by analyzing light absorption patterns. Crystal-based sensors, such as quartz crystal microbalances, detect changes in frequency due to gas adsorption, providing sensitive measurements for various applications.</p></p>
