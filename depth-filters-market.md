<p><strong>The Depth Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom-In–Top-Out Mode (Inline Configuration)</li><li>Bottom-In–Bottom-Out Mode (T-Configuration)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Depth filters are categorized into market types based on their flow configurations. In the Bottom-In–Top-Out mode (Inline Configuration), fluid enters from the bottom and exits from the top, allowing for efficient space utilization and straightforward installation, often used in continuous processes. Conversely, the Bottom-In–Bottom-Out mode (T-Configuration) allows fluid to enter and exit from the bottom, promoting even distribution and improved filtration efficiency, suitable for batch processing applications. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the application needs.</p></p>
