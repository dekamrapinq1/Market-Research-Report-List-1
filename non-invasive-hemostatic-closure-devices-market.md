<p><p>The Non-Invasive Hemostatic Closure Devices market includes three key types: closure strips, tissue adhesives, and sutures. Closure strips are adhesive devices that secure skin edges together, promoting fast healing without the need for invasive procedures. Tissue adhesives are specialized glues that bond tissue surfaces, offering quick and effective closure with minimal discomfort. Sutures are traditional methods of stitching tissue together, providing strong support for larger or deeper wounds. Together, these devices enhance patient comfort and recovery in wound management.</p></p>
