<p><p>The ship lighting system market is characterized by three main types: Halogen lights, Xenon lights, and LED lights. Halogen lights are popular for their bright illumination and affordability, often used for interior and exterior applications. Xenon lights deliver a high-intensity light output with improved energy efficiency, making them ideal for harsh marine conditions. LED lights feature long lifespans, low energy consumption, and versatility, increasingly favored for various ship lighting needs due to their durability and low maintenance requirements.</p></p>
