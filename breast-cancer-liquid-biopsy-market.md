<p><strong>The Breast Cancer Liquid Biopsy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulating Tumor Cells (CTCs)</li><li>Cell-Free DNA (CfDNA)</li><li>Extracellular Vesicles (EVs)</li><li>Other Circulating Biomarkers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The breast cancer liquid biopsy market is categorized into four main types: Circulating Tumor Cells (CTCs), which are intact cancer cells found in the bloodstream; Cell-Free DNA (CfDNA), fragments of DNA shed by tumors into circulation; Extracellular Vesicles (EVs), small membrane-bound particles that carry proteins and genetic material from tumors; and Other Circulating Biomarkers, which include various proteins and metabolites linked to cancer. These advancements facilitate non-invasive cancer diagnosis, monitoring treatment response, and assessing recurrence.</p></p>
