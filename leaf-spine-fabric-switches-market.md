<p><p>The Leaf-Spine Fabric Switch architecture is a network design model that enhances data center scalability and performance. The Leaf Switch acts as the access layer, connecting end devices like servers and storage directly. In contrast, the Spine Switch serves as the core, interconnecting multiple Leaf Switches to facilitate efficient data routing with low latency. This architecture enables high bandwidth and redundancy, making it ideal for large-scale data centers and cloud environments, ensuring robust connectivity and minimal bottlenecks.</p></p>
