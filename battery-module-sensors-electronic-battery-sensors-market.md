<p><p>The Battery Module Sensors market focuses on electronic battery sensors that monitor and manage battery performance. Two primary communication protocols used in this market are LIN (Local Interconnect Network) and CAN (Controller Area Network). LIN is a lower-cost, simpler protocol suited for less complex applications, typically for smaller sensors. In contrast, CAN supports higher data rates and greater functionality, making it ideal for more sophisticated battery management systems, particularly in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
