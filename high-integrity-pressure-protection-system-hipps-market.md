<p><p>A High-Integrity Pressure Protection System (HIPPS) is a safety system designed to prevent overpressure conditions in industrial processes. It comprises four main market types: Field Initiators, which detect pressure changes; Logic Solvers, that process the signals and make decisions; Valves, which are activated to control or shut off fluid flow; and Actuators, responsible for executing the commands of the logic solver by operating the valves. Together, these components ensure safe operations by mitigating overpressure risks effectively.</p></p>
