<p><strong>The Soil Moisture Sensing Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Soil Moisture Sensing</li><li>Soil Water Potential Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soil moisture sensing technology market encompasses two primary types: volumetric soil moisture sensing and soil water potential sensing. Volumetric soil moisture sensors measure the amount of water present in the soil as a percentage of its total volume, providing critical data for agricultural and environmental applications. In contrast, soil water potential sensors gauge the energy status of water in the soil, indicating how readily plants can absorb moisture. Both types play essential roles in optimizing irrigation and enhancing crop management.</p></p>
