<p><p>The flavored cheese market is divided into two primary types: natural cheese and processed cheese. In contrast, processed cheese is manufactured by blending natural cheese with emulsifiers and additives, providing a smooth texture and shelf stability, and is frequently flavored to appeal to diverse consumer preferences. Both types cater to growing demands for innovative cheese varieties.</p></p>
