<p><p>The Electronic Shelf Label (ESL) System is a digital pricing solution used by retailers to display product prices and information on electronic displays instead of traditional paper labels. ESLs enhance the shopping experience by providing customers with up-to-date information while enabling retailers to reduce labor costs associated with manual label changes.</p><p>The market for ESL systems is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing adoption of automation in retail, the need for efficient inventory management, and advancements in display technology.
